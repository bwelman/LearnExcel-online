Accuracy of forecasts

The difference between the actual value and the predicted value is called the forecast error. To develop forecast models you had to minimize these forecast errors. There are several measures developed to assess accuracy. You can't just consider one of these as the best. In general, the smaller the number, the better.

The measures available in Excel are:

RMSE
Root Mean Square Error
MASE
Mean Absolute Scaled Error
SMAPE
Symmetric Mean Absolute Percentage Error
MAE
Mean Absolute Percentage Error
