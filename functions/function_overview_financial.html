Overview Financial Functions

An overview of some functions for loan and depreciation.

Depreciation functions

Method Function
Linear, straight line depreciation method SLN(cost, salvage, life)
Fixed declining balance method. DB(cost, salvage, life, period, [month])
Double Declining Balance method. DDB(cost, salvage, life, period, [factor])
Sum-Of-Years-Digits method. SYD(cost, salvage, life, per)
Variable declining balance method. VDB(cost, salvage, life, start_period, end_period, [factor], [no_switch])

Payments

Method Function
Constant periodic payment to pay off a loan PMT(rate, nper, pv, [fv], [type])
Interest paid during a specific period of a loan ISPMT(rate, per, nper, pv)
Number of periods to pay off a loan or investment. NPER(rate, pmt, pv, [fv], [type])
Payment on the principal PPMT(rate, per, nper, pv, [fv], [type])

Investments

Method Function
Present value of an investment. PV(rate, nper, pmt, [fv], [type])
Future value of an investment. FV(rate, nper, [pmt], [pv], [type])

Interest Rate

Method Function
Interest rate required to pay off a loan. RATE(nper, pmt, pv, [fv], [type], [guess])
Internal rate of return of a series of periodic cash flows. IRR(values,[guess])
