Task: Renaming worksheet

Instructions on how to rename a worksheet.

t is recommended to give meaningful names to worksheets instead of the generated default names like Sheet1, Sheet2, Sheet3, ....

  1. Rename a worksheet in one of the following ways:
    • Right click on the tab name of the worksheet and then choose Rename.
    • Double click on the tab name of the worksheet.
    In both cases the name will be selected.
  2. Type the new name, closing with Enter.
