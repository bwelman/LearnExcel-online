About Chart Types

The basic types of charts.

Despite a large number of chart types, there are essentially only a limited number of basic types.

All these basic types have many variants so you can get confused quickly at first. It is important to choose a good chart type for visualizing the data. Ask yourself questions like: What should the chart tell? What facts are being compared?
